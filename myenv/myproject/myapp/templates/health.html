How to Choose the Right Health Insurance Plan

When selecting a health insurance policy, consider these important factors:

  • Monthly premium costs versus out-of-pocket expenses
  • Provider networks and whether your preferred doctors are included
  • Coverage for prescription medications you regularly take
  • Deductible amounts and how they affect your budget
  • Additional benefits like telehealth, mental health services, and wellness programs

Understanding Health Insurance Terms

Navigate the complex world of health insurance with these key definitions:

  • Premium: The amount you pay monthly for your insurance coverage
  • Deductible: The amount you must pay before your insurance begins to pay
  • Copay: A fixed amount you pay for a covered service
  • Coinsurance: The percentage of costs you pay after meeting your deductible
  • Out-of-pocket maximum: The most you'll pay during a policy period before your insurance pays 100%
