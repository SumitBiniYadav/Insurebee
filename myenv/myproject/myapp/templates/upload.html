Document Upload Help

What file formats are accepted?
We accept JPG, JPEG, PNG, and PDF files. Please ensure your documents are clear and all information is legible.
How long will document verification take?
Most documents are verified within 1-2 business days. You will receive an email notification once your documents are approved.
What if I don't have all the documents now?
You can save your progress and return later. However, your policy will only be activated once all required documents are submitted and approved.
